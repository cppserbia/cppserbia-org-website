# The Story of C++

Join us for a special community screening of **[The Story of C++](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lI7tMxzSJ7w)** — the documentary that traces the journey of C++ from its origins to its lasting impact on the programming world.

This is a relaxed, in-person get-together. Come hang out with fellow C++ enthusiasts, watch the documentary together, and stay for drinks and conversation afterward.
