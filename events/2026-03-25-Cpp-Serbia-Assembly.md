# C++ Serbia Assembly

### C++ Srbija: Skupština (C++ Србија: Скупштина)

This is not your regular meetup — there will be no technical talk this time. Instead, we are inviting you to a **community gathering** where we sit down together and talk about C++ Serbia itself.

Whether you have been with us since the very first meetup or you just discovered us, this is your chance to meet the people behind the community, hear what we have been up to, and help shape what comes next.

## What to Expect

* **Our journey so far** — A look back at C++ Serbia's milestones, from early meetups to where we are today.
* **Plans and vision for the future** — What we are working on and where we want to take the community.
* **How to get involved** — Ways you can contribute, whether it is giving a talk, helping organise events, or something else entirely.
* **Open floor Q&A** — Your questions, ideas, and feedback. This is a conversation, not a presentation.

## Who Should Attend

Everyone is welcome. You do not need to be a C++ expert, a regular attendee, or even a programmer. If you are curious about what we do and want to be part of it, come say hello.
