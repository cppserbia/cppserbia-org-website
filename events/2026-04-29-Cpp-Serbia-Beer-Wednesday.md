# C++ Serbia Beer Wednesday

Hi everyone!

We are inviting you to **C++ Serbia Beer Wednesday**!

This is not your regular meetup — there will be no technical talk this time. Instead, we are inviting you to a **community gathering** over a cold beer (or two).

Topic of the evening: **How many beers cause undefined behavior?**

## What to Expect

- **Casual conversation** — No slides, no agenda, no microphones. Just C++ folks hanging out.
- **Meet the community** — Whether you are a regular or this is your first time, come say hello.

## Who Should Attend

If you love C++, or you love beer, or you simply love hanging out with C++ developers (?!), you are welcome!
