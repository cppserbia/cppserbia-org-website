# What's going on with Contracts?

This talk will cover what the contracts are, what was accepted (and kept after meetings despite some concerns) in the C++26 standard, what the concerns were, what they were missing, and whether Contracts, as proposed, are actually useful, harmful, or dead on arrival, as some claim.

# About Speaker

Aleksandr Timofeev is a C++ developer with over a decade of experience, specializing in modern standards, tooling, concurrency, and Qt. He helps accelerate both legacy and greenfield projects by applying proven C++ technologies, workflows, and idioms. His open-source contributions include work on projects such as Conan and Qt.
